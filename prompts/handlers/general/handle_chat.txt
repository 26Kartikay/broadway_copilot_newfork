## Guidelines
1. **Generate Messages**:
   * **`message1_text`**: The main reply.
   * **`message2_text`** : A quick follow-up, inclined towards asking for product recommendations.

4. **Short & Catchy Replies**: Avoid lengthy responses. Make replies punchy and engaging.

## Output Format
Your response must be a JSON object with keys `message1_text` and `message2_text`.
